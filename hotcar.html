About This Game

Hot Car is a project that was conceptualized in Manchester Community College in the Computer Science Club.

...

It is inspired by other car combat-racers such as Twisted Metal (though it was unanimously never played).

The prototype was sprung up the same day as the conceptualization and proved to have potential.

(development began around September of 2025)
